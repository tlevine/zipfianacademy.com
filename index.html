What We Teach

Statistics

Learn the fundamentals of statistics and the theory behind the science to develop a strong foundation for the analysis of data. Apply this knowledge to extract meaning from data and separate the signal from the noise.

Visualization

The most significant insight is only as good as your ability to communicate it to others. Learn how to effectively visualize data to provide poignant and interactive displays, allowing you to tell your data story.

Storage

All Data is important, whether big or small. Learn how (and why) to choose the right datastore for the job, from one table in MySQL to an HBase cluster, and never lose a datum again.

Query

Knowing how to distill answers from a heap of data can make all the difference. Learn how to query your data efficiently to drill down into hidden insights.

Exploration

Finding answers is impossible until you know the right questions to ask. And you cannot ask questions until you know your data intimately. Learn how to interactively explore the domain of your dataset.

Machine Learning

Leveraging the computational power of computers can provide predictive insights that lie beyond the limits of human ability... there is no need to invent the future if you can predict it.

Ingestion

Choose efficient data collection techniques and identify the right information source for your analysis. Whether that be from a streaming API or parsing Apache log files.

Scrubbing

Data scientists spend ~80% of their time simply getting their data in the proper format. Cleaning data is a unavoidable but extremely important skill to know (and know well).

Optimization

Once you have built your data pipeline, making it run smoothly is no easy task. Learn how to scale your collection, storage, and analysis to handle data that grows without bounds.

Why We Teach

People create Value

More than any other time in history, we face an unprecedented growth in the amount of data stored and processed. As of 2012, it is estimated that 2.5 quintillion (2.5x10^18) bytes are created every day. Out of the necessity to make sense of this mountain of data, new techniques and technologies have been developed at companies such as Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter. As the industry begins to see the untapped value in their own data warehouses, the need for people who understand these new technologies is greater than ever.

Enter Data Science -- a new discipline bringing together statistics, advanced computing, data engineering, visualization, business acumen, and the scientific method. Using these skills, Data Scientists distill patterns and insights from rich data sources and manage data on a scale previously unimaginable. Our mission is to enable the next generation of Data Scientists to leverage these technologies to unlock the true promise of Big Data. In this 12-week intensive course, students will hear from industry leaders, work with each other in hands-on labs, complete capstone projects analyzing novel datasets, and garner the skills necessary to succeed in the new field of Data Science. We provide extensive interview preparation and match students with prospective employers during our hiring day.

Education is our Future

Technological innovation advances forward not by keeping others down, but by hoisting each other up onto the shoulders of giants. Our future rests on our efficacy at transferring knowledge from one generation to the next. At Zipfian Academy, we are dedicated to giving our students the knowledge and skills to become successful Data Scientists, and distill meaningful information from the enormous data in our world.
